4 YEARS AGO…

The idea for DiMarios was born in a Dublin Pub in the wee hours. The dream was to team the expert pizza making skills learnt in Naples with the best of Irish Ingredients.

OUR TEAM GREW!!!

Our team has grown over time, with members from Cork, Dublin, Italy and around the world all offering their own flavour and culture to the party.

OUR FIRST RESTAURANT

All the hard work has paid off with our first restaurant is about to open. Looking forward to seeing you all there!
