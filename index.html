OUR ETHOS

We are Dublin’s home to Sourdough Pizza.

Our mantra is “No fuss, just pizza” because we believe in doing one thing and doing it well. A DiMario’s pizza cooks in 90 seconds but the dough took years to perfect, hours to make and two days to rise before making that pie.

This is fast food, done slow. Done right.
